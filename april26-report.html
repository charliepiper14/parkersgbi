Mobile: Dominant Volume, Higher CPA

Mobile carries 66.7% of spend and delivers 57.7% of conversions at $5.50 CPA, $1.80 above the Computer CPA. Bid adjustments are queued for May once Sales PMax stabilises, with a likely negative adjustment of 10 to 15% on Mobile to rebalance toward higher converting devices.

Desktop: Quiet Efficiency Engine

Computer takes 27.6% of spend but generates 35.5% of conversions at $3.70 CPA. The May plan applies a positive Computer bid adjustment alongside the Mobile correction to skew the algorithm toward proven efficiency.

Device Strategy for May 2026

Mobile bid adjustments held back through April to avoid disrupting the Sales PMax bidding rebuild. Once stabilised in early May, Mobile minus 10 to 15% and Computer plus 10% will be applied in a single pass to rebalance device economics without resetting learning phases.
